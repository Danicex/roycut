Who We Are

RoyCut is a Lagos-based network of elite barbers delivering premium home haircut services across Lekki, Victoria Island, Ikoyi, Ikeja, and beyond.

We rose during the COVID-19 pandemic by bringing professional grooming directly to our clients — safely, reliably, and without compromise. That moment shaped our standard.

Today, RoyCut stands for precision, comfort, and consistency. No queues. No stress. Just book, and we’ll be there.

Our Story

When the pandemic forced barbershops to close, we saw an opportunity. People still needed quality grooming — and many preferred the safety and convenience of home appointments. RoyCut was born from that insight.

Today, we serve discerning clients across Lagos. Our barbers are carefully selected, extensively trained, and deeply committed to excellence. Every appointment is a chance to exceed expectations.
